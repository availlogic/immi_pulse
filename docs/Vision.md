# ImmiPulse - Project Vision

## Project Vision
ImmiPulse is a highly timely, multi-perspective, vertical web application dedicated to the immigration and global mobility space. Unlike traditional database comparison tools, ImmiPulse is centered on curated news and policy updates, aggregating authoritative and diverse global sources to help users get precise and comprehensive immigration dynamics in real-time.

## Core Principles
- **Timeliness First**: Daily updates ensure that key information is captured and delivered without overnight delay.
- **Curated Delivery ("Less is More")**: To prevent information overload, the system delivers a maximum of 10 high-quality, high-relevance core news items to each user daily.
- **Transparency and Trust**: Every curated item must include the original source link, allowing users to verify facts and make informed judgments.

## Target Users
- **Prospective Immigrants & Expatriates**: Individuals and families planning or navigating the immigration process who need reliable, prompt information.
- **Relocation Professionals & Enthusiasts**: Industry observers, relocation managers, or individuals tracking global mobility trends.

## User Problems
- **Information Fragmentation**: Policy updates and analysis are scattered across hundreds of official portals, news websites, law firms, and migration agencies.
- **Information Overload**: Endless duplicate articles and low-quality summaries make it difficult to identify critical policy changes.
- **Lack of Trust**: Many news items are recycled by intermediaries without clear references or original sources.
- **Time Sensitivity**: Missing a deadline or policy adjustment (e.g., quota cuts or sudden draws) can have life-altering impacts.

## Product Goals
- Provide a unified, clean portal for global immigration and mobility news.
- Keep users updated on key destination policies with high temporal efficiency.
- Deliver high-value, deduplicated, and contextualized content rather than raw, noisy feeds.

## Core Capabilities
- **Multi-Source Aggregation**: Comprehensive tracking across official channels (immigration bureaus, government gazettes, border agencies), mainstream media, and professional immigration firms/law offices.
- **Customized Subscriptions**: User accounts with personalized jurisdiction selections (e.g., United Kingdom, United States, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore). Defaults to global coverage if no specific destination is selected.
- **Two-Dimensional Tagging**:
  - **Jurisdiction Tags**: Multi-jurisdiction tagging to support comparative updates.
  - **Feature Tags**: Category tags based on lifestyle and requirements (`Raising a Family`, `Education`, `Retirement`, `Vacation`, `Culture Inclusion`).
- **Keyword Alerts**: Push notifications triggered by user-defined keyword and jurisdiction combinations (e.g., "policy change" or "quota reduction").

## Out-of-Scope
- Direct visa application processing or legal consultation services.
- Social networking or community forums between users.
- Universal database comparison of immigration criteria (the product focuses primarily on dynamic news, updates, and professional commentary/interpretations).
